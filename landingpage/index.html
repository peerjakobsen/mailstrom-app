You've tried inbox zero. You've tried filters. You've tried ignoring it. Nothing sticks because the problem isn't discipline — it's that cleaning up email one-by-one doesn't scale.

How it works

01

Connect via OAuth

Sign in with your Google account. Mailstrom never sees your password — just secure, revocable OAuth access.

02

Analyze by sender

Mailstrom scans your inbox and groups every email by sender domain. Instantly see who's flooding you.

03

Bulk clean

Select senders, hit delete. Unsubscribe and remove hundreds of emails in a single click.

Features

Sender Tree View

See your entire inbox grouped by domain and sender. Instantly spot who sends you the most email — and how much.

One-Click Bulk Delete

Select one sender or ten. Delete hundreds of emails at once instead of clicking through them one by one.

Smart Unsubscribe

Detect unsubscribe links automatically. Clean up and unsubscribe in a single workflow — no more hunting for tiny footer links.

Privacy-First

Everything runs locally on your Mac. No cloud servers, no data collection. Your email never leaves your device.

Your email stays on your Mac.

Mailstrom is a native macOS app. It connects to Gmail through OAuth — meaning it never sees your password and you can revoke access anytime. All processing happens locally. There are no cloud servers, no analytics, no data leaving your machine.

No cloud servers OAuth — no password access Revoke anytime

Before you clean up

Mailstrom is provided as-is. Deleted emails are moved to Gmail's Trash, where they can be recovered for 30 days — after that, they're gone for good. Always review your selections before deleting. Mentilead is not responsible for any emails removed through the app.

You can revoke Mailstrom's access anytime via your Google account settings.
