# Mailstrom - Landing Page

## Product

- **Name**: Mailstrom
- **Type**: Desktop utility / Developer tool
- **Tagline**: Bulk-clean your Gmail inbox by sender — see who's flooding you, unsubscribe, and delete in one click.

## Target Audience

Tech-savvy users drowning in email. People with 5,000+ unread emails who've given up on inbox zero. Developers, founders, productivity nerds.

## What It Does

A free macOS app that:

1. Connects to Gmail via OAuth (never sees your password)
2. Analyzes your inbox and groups emails by sender in a tree view
3. Shows who's flooding you (domain → sender → count)
4. Lets you bulk-delete and unsubscribe in one action

## Page Sections

1. **Hero** - Headline + tagline + "Download for macOS" CTA (coming soon state) + app screenshot
2. **Problem** - The email overwhelm pain point
3. **How It Works** - 3 steps: Connect → Analyze → Clean
4. **Features** - Sender tree view, bulk actions, unsubscribe detection, privacy-first (local only)
5. **Privacy/Trust** - Data never leaves your device, OAuth means no password access
6. **CTA** - Download button + "Coming to Windows/Linux"
7. **Footer** - Made by Mentilead, links
